Commonly Asked Questions


Can I file taxes online?

Yes, most countries allow e-filing through their tax authority’s website or approved software.

What documents are needed for filing taxes?

Proof of income (pay stubs, W-2, Form 16), investment proofs, tax deductions, and previous year’s return.

What is an audit, and why am I selected?

A tax audit reviews your return for accuracy. Random selection or discrepancies often trigger audits.

How do I check my refund status?

Use the tax authority's online portal or helpline to track your refund.

India: ITR Forms

India has a range of Income Tax Return forms for individuals, businesses, and organizations. Common forms include:

  • ITR-1: For individuals with income up to ₹50 lakh from salary, one house property, and other sources.
  • ITR-2: For individuals/HUFs with income from salary, capital gains, or more than one property.
  • ITR-3: For income from business or profession.
  • ITR-4: For presumptive income under sections 44AD, 44ADA, and 44AE.

United States: Tax Forms

The IRS provides different forms for individuals and entities, including:

  • Form 1040: For individual taxpayers.
  • Form W-2: For reporting wages and salaries.
  • Form 1099: For reporting income from sources like freelancing or investments.
Access IRS forms →

United Kingdom: Tax Forms

In the UK, HMRC offers forms for various tax needs, such as:

  • SA100: For Self Assessment tax returns.
  • P60: Annual summary of pay and tax for employees.
  • P45: For employees leaving a job.
Find UK tax forms →

Canada: Tax Forms

Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) provides the following forms:

  • T1: General income tax and benefit return for individuals.
  • T2: Corporation income tax return.
  • TD1: Personal tax credits return.
Access CRA forms →

Australia: Tax Forms

The Australian Taxation Office (ATO) offers forms such as:

  • Individual Tax Return: For personal income tax filing.
  • Business Activity Statement (BAS): For businesses reporting GST and PAYG.
  • TFN Declaration: To declare your tax file number.
Find ATO forms →

Germany: Tax Forms

The Federal Central Tax Office (BZSt) provides forms such as:

  • ESt 1 A: For annual income tax return.
  • Umsatzsteuer-Voranmeldung: VAT advance return.
  • Kindergeld Form: For child benefits.
Access German tax forms →

South Africa: Tax Forms

South African Revenue Service (SARS) offers forms like:

  • ITR12: Individual income tax return.
  • ITR14: For companies.
  • VAT201: VAT return form.
